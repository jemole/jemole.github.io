Jesús Moreno León Computational Thinking (CT) researcher

After more than a decade as a Computer Science teacher, I am focused on research in CT:

  • I run Programamos (We Code) a Spanish non-profit organization which promotes the development of CT from early ages;
  • work on my PhD thesis on CT at Universidad Rey Juan Carlos, where we develop Dr. Scratch ;
  • work as a research assistant at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid in the eMadrid Project;
  • and collaborate with EU Code Week as the Spanish national ambassador.
