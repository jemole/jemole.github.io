Jesús Moreno León

Technology-enhanced learning · Computational Thinking
(+34) 913778324 · jml@jemole.me

I work at the National Institute for Educational Technologies and Teacher Training (Ministry of Education, Spain), where I lead CodeINTEF, a project to include computational thinking skills in the national curriculum. I am also part of the the KGBL3 research group at Universidad Rey Juan Carlos, where we created Dr. Scratch. You can read about this and other research on the development of computational thinking skills through programming with Scratch in my PhD thesis. Whenever I have time, I volunteer at Programamos (We Code), a non-profit organization supporting educators in bringing computer programming to schools.
