Jesús Moreno León Computational Thinking researcher

Presentation

After more than a decade as a Computer Science teacher, I am focused on research in Computational Thinking (CT):

  • I run Programamos, We Code, a Spanish non-profit organization which promotes the development of CT from early ages,
  • work on my PhD thesis on CT at Universidad Rey Juan Carlos, where we develop Dr. Scratch,
  • work as a research assistant at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid,
  • and collaborate with EU Code Week as a Spanish ambassador.
